Clean air

Clean air was already an important topic to consider. It is required for a healthy life to live in an area where you can breathe healthy air. Currently, with the pandemic, the requirements for clean air have skyrocketed. People after COVID have little lung capacity left and if the air quality is low they could not get the right amount of oxygen they need.

AirQualityDashboard helps you with this!

To get a better understanding of how you can improve the quality of air around you you first need to understand what makes good air. Our dashboard will explain what factors influence the air quality in what way. You will understand what creates clean air and you will be able to improve the quality of the air around yourself.

AirQualitySensorKit

The sensor kit will actively measure the air quality in the room of your choosing, not only will you get live statistics of the current situation in your area. You will also get informed if the quality of air becomes a danger to your health and how to change it. To sensor kit, is the perfect tool to make sure you are breathing the air you deserve!
